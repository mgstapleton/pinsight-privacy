Privacy Policy

App: Pinsight
Last updated: April 26, 2026

Pinsight ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use the Pinsight mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: email address and password when you register.
  • Profile information: display name, handicap index, handedness, and preferred distance units — all optional.
  • Golf data: courses, rounds, hole-by-hole scores, putts, fairway and green statistics, and mistake notes you record in the App.

Information generated automatically

  • AI coaching insights: aggregated statistics from your rounds are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate personalised coaching insights. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests beyond your statistical data.
  • Token usage metadata: we record the number of AI API tokens consumed per request for operational monitoring only.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To store and display your golf rounds and statistics.
  • To generate personalised AI coaching insights using your anonymised statistical data.
  • To improve the App's features and fix bugs.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS). All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Row-Level Security policies ensure each user can only access their own data. Authentication is managed by Supabase Auth.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – database and authentication provider. Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API – AI insight generation. Anonymised statistical data only. Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the App's Settings screen. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.

6. Children's Privacy

Pinsight is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. You can exercise many of these rights directly within the App (Settings → Delete Account). For other requests, please contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via the App or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
