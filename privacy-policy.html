Privacy Policy

App: Pinsight
Last updated: April 26, 2026

Pinsight ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use the Pinsight mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: email address and authentication credentials managed by Supabase Auth when you register.
  • Profile information: display name, handicap index, handedness, and preferred distance units — all optional.
  • Golf data: courses, rounds, hole-by-hole scores, putts, fairway and green statistics, and mistake notes you record in the App.

Information generated automatically

  • AI coaching insights: limited golf performance statistics from your rounds may be sent to Google's Gemini API to generate personalised coaching insights. We do not intentionally include your name, email address, or password in these requests.
  • Token usage metadata: we may record the number of AI API tokens consumed per request for operational monitoring only.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To store and display your golf rounds and statistics.
  • To generate personalised AI coaching insights using limited golf performance data.
  • To improve the App's features, reliability, and user experience.
  • To fix bugs, monitor performance, and prevent misuse of the App.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase, which is hosted on cloud infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit using TLS. Supabase provides database security controls, and Row-Level Security policies are used to help ensure each user can only access their own data. Authentication is managed by Supabase Auth.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – database and authentication provider. Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API – AI insight generation using limited golf performance data. Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at support@stapletondigital.com. Once completed, deletion is permanent and irreversible.

6. Children's Privacy

Pinsight is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via the App or by email where appropriate. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
