Features

Everything your game needs

From the first tee to the final putt — Pinsight captures everything and turns it into actionable coaching.

📋

Hole-by-Hole Scorecard

Record scores, putts, fairways, GIR, up-and-down attempts, and penalties for every hole. A live scorecard keeps your round front and centre.

🎯

Mistake Tracking

Log execution and strategy mistakes per hole — poor strike, wrong club, course management errors — with optional notes in your own words.

📊

Stats Dashboard

Scoring average, GIR %, putts per round, fairway %, up & down %, and shots lost — all in one clean dashboard.

📈

Strokes Gained Putting

See how your putting compares to your peer group. Track first putt distance and pinpoint whether your weakness is lag putting or short-range execution.

🏌️

Course Management

Add any course with per-tee par values and stroke indexes. Search thousands of courses or create your own.

📅

Round History

Browse all past rounds with correct totals, putts, and performance trends. See how your game has evolved over time.
